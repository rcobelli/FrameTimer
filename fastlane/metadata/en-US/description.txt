Slow motion video analysis in a fast paced world!

Use this free app to time actions that occur in minute moments of time. Use the Slow-Motion capability of your device in the default camera app to film the event. Switch to this app and analyze your video frame by frame with time stamps accurate up to a 50th of a second!

If you are tracking a specific event, turn on key-frames (tap the red button in the top left corner) and advance to your next critical frame. The app will display the elapsed time between key-frames at the bottom of the screen.

Completely free and no internet connection required. Download now!
